Frequently Asked Questions

Where are SugarBomb products manufactured? +
All SugarBomb products are manufactured in our 20,000 sqm GMP and Halal certified facility in Klang, Selangor, Malaysia. We control every step from R&D to packaging.
How long do SugarBomb fragrances last? +
Our fragrances are formulated to last approximately 8–24 hours depending on the product line and concentration. They are specifically optimized for tropical humid climates.
Are your products Halal certified? +
Yes, all our manufacturing facilities and processes are Halal certified. Our formulations are Muslim-friendly and comply with Islamic standards.
How can I become a SugarBomb distributor? +
We welcome distributor partnerships. Please fill out the contact form above with "Distributor Partnership" as the subject, and our team will reach out with details on joining our extensive network.
Do you offer OEM / private label services? +
Yes! Our factory supports OEM and private label fragrance manufacturing. With 6 registered chemists and full production capabilities, we can bring your brand's fragrance vision to life. Contact our B2B team at b2b@sugarbombgroup.com.
