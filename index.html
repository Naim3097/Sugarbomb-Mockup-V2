Brand Story

At SugarBomb, we believe true beauty is incomplete without the perfect fragrance. Founded in 2015, our name embodies our mission: "Sugar" represents the sweetness of life, and "Bomb" signifies our explosive impact on the perfume industry. We are dedicated to crafting unforgettable scents that become an essential, invisible accessory in every space and on every person.

Guided by our core values — Dare to Dream, Accountability, Growth, Discipline, and Teamwork — we transform dreams into reality, setting new standards in the fragrance world. At SugarBomb, we do not just create scents; we create experiences that linger.
