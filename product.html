Description Scent Profile Reviews (128)

Hush Lush is SugarBomb's signature bestseller from the Luscious Collection. Inspired by tropical Malaysian botanicals, this fragrance opens with a burst of fresh citrus and gradually unfolds into a heart of lush florals, settling into a warm, musky base that lingers for up to 24 hours.

Specifically formulated for tropical humid climates, Hush Lush is designed to perform in Malaysia's weather while maintaining its scent integrity throughout the day. Our Muslim-friendly formulation uses only halal-certified ingredients, manufactured in our GMP-certified facility in Klang, Selangor.

Key Features

  • Long-lasting: 8–24 hours wear time
  • Climate-adapted for tropical humidity
  • Halal & GMP certified
  • Muslim-friendly formulation
  • Available in 50ml and 100ml
