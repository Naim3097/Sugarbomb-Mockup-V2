The Story of SugarBomb

At SugarBomb, we believe true beauty is incomplete without the perfect fragrance. Founded in 2015, our name embodies our mission: "Sugar" represents the sweetness of life, and "Bomb" signifies our explosive impact on the perfume industry.

We are dedicated to crafting unforgettable scents that become an essential, invisible accessory in every space and on every person. What started as a vision in Malaysia has grown into a multi-brand fragrance ecosystem reaching 6 countries across 2 continents.

At SugarBomb, we do not just create scents; we create experiences that linger.

Our Journey

2015
SugarBomb founded in Malaysia with a mission to make quality fragrance accessible.
2017
Launched first signature collection. Expanded distributor network across Peninsular Malaysia.
2019
Opened 20,000 sqm manufacturing facility in Klang, Selangor. Achieved GMP and Halal certification.
2021
International expansion — entered Brunei, Singapore, and Indonesia markets.
2023
Reached 5 million bottles annual production. Participated in Beautyworld Middle East (Dubai).
2024
Expanded to UK and Middle East. Launched Blast sports fragrance line and SugarBomb mobile app.
2026
250+ scents across all categories. 6 countries, 2 continents. Leading Malaysia's fragrance revolution.
